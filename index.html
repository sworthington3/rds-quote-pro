Quote & customer details

Standard assumptions — included on every quote

  • Pricing is valid for seven (7) days from the quote date.
  • Installation scheduling is subject to equipment and product availability.
  • Customer will provide adequate electrical power, network connectivity, internet access, and suitable workspace where required.
  • Equipment warranties are provided by the manufacturer unless otherwise stated.
  • Software licensing and subscription renewals begin upon activation.
  • Taxes, permits, freight, tariffs, and third-party fees are included only when specifically listed.
  • Work requested outside the defined scope may require a written change order.
  • Training is limited to the services specifically listed in this proposal.

Standard exclusions — included on every quote

  • Electrical work and electrical upgrades
  • Low-voltage cabling and network infrastructure
  • Construction, remodeling, or site modifications
  • Data migration unless specifically quoted
  • Third-party software or services not listed
  • Permits, inspection fees, and governmental charges
  • Support for customer-supplied equipment unless specifically identified
